# ADR Template

Use this template for new Architecture Decision Records.

```markdown
# {short title of solved problem and solution}

## Status

{Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [ADR-005](005-example.md)}

## Context

{Describe the context and problem statement. What is the issue that is motivating
this decision or change? What forces are at play (technical, business, political,
social)?}

## Decision

{Describe the decision that was made and the rationale behind it. State the
decision clearly and concisely.}

## Consequences

### Positive

- {List positive outcomes}

### Negative

- {List negative outcomes or trade-offs}

### Neutral

- {List neutral observations}
```
